HOSUR

Hosur is an industrial city in Tamil Nadu, located near the Karnataka border, known for its booming manufacturing sector, especially in electronics and electric vehicles. It has transformed into a major industrial hub with thousands of small and large companies, attracting a skilled workforce. The city is also a municipal corporation, situated on the Ponnaiyar River, and offers a pleasant climate, leading it to be called 'Little England'. Hosur is one of Tamil Nadu's key industrial centers, hosting major companies like Ashok Leyland, Titan, and TVS, with significant investments in electric vehicle manufacturing. It is located approximately 40 kilometers southeast of Bengaluru, making it a crucial hub for trade and commerce. Known for its cool and pleasant weather, often compared to that of England, earning it the nickname "Little England" during the British regime. The city has historical sites like the Chandra Choodeswarar Temple and natural spots such as Muthanallur Lake and Brahma Hills.
